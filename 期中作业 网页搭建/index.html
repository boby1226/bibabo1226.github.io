Steps of my imagination

Step One

In the dark night, there is a magical world in the closed eyes. I can't judge beauty or ugliness. It always looks like waves, layer by layer, wave by wave.They are usually gorgeous black and dark purple, with a little cobalt blue.

Step Two

I slowly opened my eyes, the impression of the waves weakened, but there were stars in the universe. They are equally dazzling, winking at me. I was so dizzy that I couldn't tell whether it was a reality or a dream.

Step Three

Until I opened my eyes completely. In fact, the black in front of us is also like the black with eyes closed. There is no doubt that I love the sea. I was born the son of the sea. I am infatuated with his majesty, surging and mystery. I want to go to the seaside again.

Opera in Minnan


The last paragraph of "overseas Chinese criticism" is selected as the main content of the creation. The two have not seen each other for seven or eight years. Ruyi has already been engaged to others. When the first love met in the past, there was no quarrel and estrangement in their hearts. They went to each other, shook hands, and said good and bad things to each other. Huang Rixing brought letters and silver dollars (that is, overseas Chinese) from people in Nanyang's hometown. He is a hero in the hearts of people in Southern Fujian. Ruyi is no exception. So this moment seems sad, but the kernel is happy.The background is the video I took in the evening in Xiamen. The waves are tender and tender. At the same time, it also points out that the background of the story is Nanyang. The lyrics are in Minnan and have special feelings. The figures are painted by hand, and the silk threads at the joints are in response to the traditional culture of puppet show in Southern Fujian. The video records people's most innocent and simple after dinner life at that time. It is also my persistent and sincere longing for my childhood, as well as my love for my hometown and the sea.
